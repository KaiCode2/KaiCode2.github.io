WWDC: A Beginner’s Guide

  Last year I was fortunate enough to win a coveted scholarship to Apple’s annual WorldWide Developers Conference. It was an amazing week full of learning, networking, coding, touring and just about everything in between. However as a first-timer it certainly seemed a bit overwhelming and felt as though I was entering uncharted lands. So, to ease that feeling for future generations I thought I’d write about my week and give some pointers and insight into the awe-inspiring week.
